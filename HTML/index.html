About Us

Ponjo Trading and Projects, trading as (PT Projects) is an integrated services provider which was established in 2012. The owner and the MD is Mr Mosemeng, whose personal funds provided the initial and continuing investment to launch the business. PT Projects provides multi-disciplinary services pertaining to most aspects of the engineering industry. This company renders services to a diverse range of clients with equal success including government departments, NGO’s and the private sector. Our services are enhanced on a progressive basis to suit market demands without compromising ethical and professional standards.

Vision

To be the pre-eminent leader in the Telecommunication industry, construction, civil and maintenance of fibre optic networks , Lan cabling Solution in design and earn reputation as an employer of choice and a sustainable empowerment magnate.

Objectives

To offer quality service at affordable prices within the stipulated timeframe.

Mission

Conquer recognition through reliable quality products, consistent service deliveries and continually grow and add stakeholder value as responsible corporate social citizens.
